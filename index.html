About Greg

I have a deep passion for technology and programming. I fell in love with programming when I began working with Programmable Logic Controllers. It was an amazing thing to be able to control how machines function and behave. The more I learned about programming, the more I discovered that I had so much more to learn. I have then begun to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript (all of which I am quite comfortable), React, Node.JS, and Postgres.

I have a Bachelors degree in Organizational Leadership and a Masters degree in Business Administration. I enjoy breaking down business ideas and needs to find ways to meet them in a logical way with maximized value added to their process.
