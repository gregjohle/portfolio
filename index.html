Meet Greg

I am a former industrial automation engineer living in Raleigh, North Carolina. I have years of experience learning and growing my skills, from military electronics repair, to programming palletized production lines and SCADA, and finally web development. These unique skills helps me to:

  • Create functional and efficient web applications
  • Work with teams to learn and grow, while producing quality work
  • Take pride in my work, while doing my part to help the whole team succeed

I love programming mainly because I love solving complex problems with logical and elegant solutions.

About Greg

I have a deep passion for technology and programming. I fell in love with programming when I began working with Programmable Logic Controllers. It was an amazing thing to be able to control how machines function and behave. The more I learned about programming, the more I discovered that I had so much more to learn. I have then begun to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript (all of which I an quite comfortable), React, Node.JS, and Postgres.

I have a Bachelors degree in Organizational Leadership and a Masters degree in Business Administration. I believe that this combination of technical experience and education gives me a unique perspective. I enjoy breaking down business ideas and needs to find ways to meet them in a logical way with maximized value added to their process.

In my free time I enjoy

  • Learning and working with Linux
  • Developing interesting machines on the Arduino and Raspberry Pi platforms
  • 3D printing

Greg's Services

I work well with others. I am either eager to learn something new, or share knowledge and techniques with others. As a full stack developer, I can help create beautiful and functional sites that function with powerful back-ends to meet and exceed client expectations.

I am looking forward to building my skills and knowledge through collaborative work with skilled developers.
